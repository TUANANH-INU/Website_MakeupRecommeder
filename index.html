Brow Magic
Using L’Oréal’s Modiface AR technology, L’Oréal Brow Magic scans the user’s face and makes recommendations for microblading, micro-shading, or filler effects.
HAPTA
HAPTA’s leveling device will be piloted with L’Oréal-owned Lancôme in 2023, first with a lipstick applicator followed by additional makeup applications in the future.
Colorsonic
The Colorsonic is a handheld device from L'Oreal that aims to make the at-home hair coloring process less messy and more consistent.
YSL Scent-Sation
YSL Scent-Sation is a state-of-the-art, in-store experience leveraging neuroscience to provide personalized fragrance advice, premiering at the Dubai Mall in the YSL Beauty Innovation Lab pop up from December 16th to 28th, 2021.
